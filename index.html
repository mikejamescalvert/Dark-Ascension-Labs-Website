About the Studio

Building a Dark Fantasy Universe

Dark Ascension Labs is an indie game studio focused on atmospheric dark fantasy experiences. We believe in deep game systems, pixel art craftsmanship, and stories that reward exploration.

Our first game, Dark Ascension, launched on iOS and Windows with a passionate community of players. Now we're expanding the universe with Thelsicker Ascends — a metroidvania that transforms the idle game's 12 stages into a hand-crafted side-scrolling adventure.

Every spell you cast, every artifact you find, and every boss you fight connects back to the same dark fantasy world.
