Features

Master the Dark Arts

A metroidvania built around the thrill of growing from a swordsman into an unstoppable arcane force.

⚔

Sword to Sorcery

Begin with nothing but a blade. Defeat bosses to awaken ancient spells — Mana Surge, Dark Harvest, Essence Storm, Time Warp, Shadow Clone, Void Pulse, and the devastating Dark Pact.

★

12 Interconnected Zones

From The Awakening's tutorial caves to the Primordial Darkness endgame. Each zone has unique enemies, secrets, and ability gates that reward exploration.

☠

12 Boss Fights

Multi-phase boss encounters that test every ability you've unlocked. Each boss guards a new power — defeat them to open paths forward.

✨

8 Ancient Artifacts

Hidden across the world, each artifact grants passive bonuses — faster spell cooldowns, increased damage, health regeneration, and more. Collect them all to unlock a hidden zone.

⚡

Tight Movement

Double jump, wall climb, and phantom dash through hazards. Responsive controls with coyote time, jump buffering, and variable jump height for precision platforming.

🎨

Connected Universe

Set in the same world as Dark Ascension. The same 12 stages, spells, and artifacts are reimagined as a side-scrolling adventure. Cross-game unlocks planned.
